Final run time:  45 minutes

The loss is a weighted sum of a negative expected reward term and a cross-entropy reconstruction term, where the expected reward is computed as the softmax-weighted sum over all possible actions, making the objective fully differentiable. Minimising it jointly maximises survival reward and enforces correct entity classification.

The negative loss is equal to:

- reward_scale × expected_reward + recon_weight × recon_loss =
- `-0.2 × 11.26 + 2.0 × ~0` ≈ `-2.25`

The recon loss collapsed to near-zero (perfect reconstruction), so the loss is dominated by the negative reward term.
